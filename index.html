I am an oceanography graduate specializing in marine climate hazards, hydrometeorological disasters, geospatial analysis, and marine pollution research. My experience includes marine heatwave detection, multi-hazard coastal vulnerability assessment, flood susceptibility modeling (HEC-RAS, AHP, MCDA), and microplastic contamination studies in the Meghna estuary and Sandwip Channel.

Currently a Research Assistant at the National Oceanographic and Maritime Institute (NOAMI), I have worked on the August 2024 extreme flood multidisciplinary analysis, flood susceptibility mapping, and coastal vulnerability projects. I aim to pursue graduate studies in marine or environmental science and contribute to climate-resilient coastal management.
