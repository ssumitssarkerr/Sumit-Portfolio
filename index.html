A Bit About Me

I am a BSc graduate in Oceanography from Noakhali Science and Technology University, passionate about the intersections of marine science, climate change, and geospatial technology. I specialize in integrating scientific research with advanced tools such as Python, R, QGIS, ArcGIS, data visualization, and applications of machine learning and AI for environmental analysis. My experience spans mapping coastal land use changes, detecting marine heatwaves, and analyzing marine pollution, transforming complex datasets into actionable insights. Beyond research, I have contributed to proposals, poster presentations, and leadership initiatives, honing my skills in communication, teamwork, and project management. My key interests include marine heatwaves, harmful algal blooms, and coastal ecosystem sustainability, with a focus on Bangladesh’s blue economy. I am eager to contribute as a marine and environmental researcher, applying my expertise in GIS, remote sensing, and data-driven environmental solutions.
